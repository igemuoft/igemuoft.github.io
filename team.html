meet Our Team

iGem Toronto is an interdisciplinary organization with divisions in three different areas

WET LAB

The wet lab team is responsible for carrying out the experiments necessary to advance our project in the laboratory.

DRY LAB

The dry lab team is responsible for carrying out the computational and mathematical design necessary to plan and advance our project.

PNP

The PnP team is responsible for investigating the social, ethical and regulatory aspects of the project and for engaging the general public with the science that we are looking at.
